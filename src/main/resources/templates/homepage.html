FAQs

We offer a variety of pets for sale, including dogs and cats. Our pets come from reputable breeders and rescue organizations to ensure they are healthy and well-cared for before being placed for adoption.

Yes, you can book a pet in advance for purchase. We offer a reservation system that allows you to secure a pet before it becomes available for adoption. Contact us for more details on the booking process.

At the moment, we do not offer shipping services for pets. Our focus is on providing a personal and secure adoption process for our pets. You will need to visit our store to adopt a pet in person.

Training your new pet is important to ensure a happy and well-behaved companion. We recommend positive reinforcement training methods. You can also enroll in training classes or seek advice from our staff on effective training techniques for dogs and cats.

Proper vaccinations are crucial for the health of your new pet. Our pets are up-to-date on core vaccinations. We will provide you with vaccination records and guidance on when to schedule follow-up shots for your pet's well-being.

Yes, we offer professional pet grooming services. Our trained groomers will provide grooming services for dogs and cats, including baths, haircuts, nail trimming, and more, to keep your pet looking and feeling their best.
